Duality, Relativity and Non-Existence: Be careful

Felix Bogdanski, since 21.4.2019

Hello.

In scientific circles, I heard people say: "Everything is relative." This statement is false, because it expresses the very absolute itself, referring to everything.

In spiritual circles, I heard people say: "Everything is dualistic, it's the Law of Polarity." This law is false, because when you apply the law to itself, it vanishes. Any universal law should be applicable to itself, without contradicting.

In intellecutal circles, I heard people say: "God does not exist. Time does not exist. I don't exist." These statements, too, are false. When you negate X existentially, you need the existence of X first to negate it.

In mathematics, I heard people say: "1 ≠ 0". Usually, they come up with proofs using the postulate itself, which violates the core principle of a solid proof. I even read papers from university professors doing this. It is false. All we can do is assume that 1 ≠ 0.

All there is, is infinite uniqueness — love logos, find god.
